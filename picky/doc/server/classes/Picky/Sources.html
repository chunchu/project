Data Sources

Currently, Picky offers the following Sources:

  • CSV (comma – or other – separated file)
  • Couch (CouchDB, key-value store)
  • DB (Databases, foremost MySQL)
  • Delicious (del.icio.us, online bookmarking service)

See also: github.com/floere/picky/wiki/Sources-Configuration

Don’t worry if your source isn’t here. Adding your own is easy: github.com/floere/picky/wiki/Contributing-sources
